Our Supporters

Sri. S.V. Arumugam

The Chairman and Managing Director and Promoter of our Company. He holds a Bachelors Degree in Science and is a qualified Chartered Accountant. He has more than two and half decades of experience in the textile Industry especially in planning procurement, execution and management. He is the Vice Chairman of Indian Wind Power Association, Chennai. He is the past Chairman of Confederation of Indian Textile Industry (CITI), an apex body for the textile industry. He is the past chairman of Southern India Mills Association (SIMA). He is a past committee member of the Indian Cotton Mills Federation (ICMF).

Sri. Tharanipathy Rajkumar

He holds a Master of Business Administration degree. He has been associated with the textile industry for over two decades as an entrepreneur and through various industry bodies like SIMA and CITI. He has also been involved in other manufacturing and service sectors for more than 20 years. He served as Chairman of The Southern India Mills’ Association (SIMA), SIMA CDRA, and the Confederation of Indian Textile Industry (CITI).

Dr. S. K. Sundararaman
